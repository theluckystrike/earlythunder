## Tier and Score Disclaimers

Tier assignments (Tier 1 through Tier 4) and composite scores (0-10) are classifications based on our proprietary methodology. They are not ratings, endorsements, or recommendations.

- A **Tier 1 (Pre-Narrative)** designation means we believe the opportunity is early and exhibits a signal pattern consistent with historical breakouts. It does not mean the opportunity will break out. Most Tier 1 entries will fail.
- A **high composite score** means multiple signals are firing positively according to our framework. It does not mean the opportunity is safe, likely to succeed, or appropriate for your portfolio.
- A **low Risk-Adjusted Conviction** score is an explicit warning that we believe the probability of total loss is elevated.
