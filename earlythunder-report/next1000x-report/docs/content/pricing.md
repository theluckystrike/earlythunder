## Pricing FAQ

**Is the free tier really free?**
Yes. No credit card required. No time limit. No feature downgrades over time. The free database view is a permanent offering. We believe showing the surface-level data builds trust and demonstrates the quality of our coverage. The paid tier exists for people who want the full analysis layer.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDC) for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

**Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
Yes. You can switch at any time from your account settings. If you switch from monthly to annual, you'll receive a prorated credit for the unused portion of your current month.

**Is there a refund policy?**
Yes. 14-day refund policy, no questions asked. If Pro doesn't deliver value within your first two weeks, email us and we'll refund the full amount. For annual plans, refunds are prorated after the 14-day window.

**What happens when my free trial ends?**
Your card is charged the standard monthly rate ($49) on day 8 unless you cancel before then. Cancellation takes one click in your account settings. There's no retention flow, no "are you sure" dialogs — you click cancel, you're cancelled.

**Can I get a team or enterprise rate?**
For teams of 5+ or enterprise licensing, contact us at team@next1000x.com. We offer volume pricing and custom data delivery.

**Does the API tier include everything in Pro?**
Yes. API subscribers get full dashboard access plus programmatic access. It's not a separate product — it's Pro with an API key on top.

**Can I increase the API rate limit?**
The standard limit of 1,000 requests/day covers most use cases. If you need higher throughput, contact us at api@next1000x.com to discuss custom limits. Pricing scales linearly.

**What if I just want the weekly email digest?**
The free tier includes a basic weekly newsletter with tier changes and new entries. The Pro-level weekly alert with full signal data, catalyst updates, and graveyard moves requires a Pro subscription. You can subscribe to the free newsletter without an account — just enter your email on the homepage.
