// Methodology

Transparency in how signals are generated and filtered.

Convergence Detection

The convergence detector cross-references data from 6 independent scanners: airdrop opportunity scanner, earnings yield scanner, stablecoin flow tracker, TVL inflow monitor, developer activity tracker, and VC investment tracker. When 2+ scanners independently flag the same protocol, a convergence event is generated.

  • Tokenless status: +25 conviction points
  • TVL growth >10% 7d: +10 points
  • Chain stablecoin inflow: +10 points
  • Active developer commits: +20 points
  • Each additional scanner hit: +5 points

Hyperliquid-Grade Earnings Yield

Named after Hyperliquid, which demonstrated that protocols earning real revenue relative to their market cap can be dramatically undervalued. The formula:

Earnings Yield = (Revenue_30d x 12) / Market Cap x 100%

Tiers: Hyperliquid-Grade (>100%), High (50-100%), Moderate (20-50%), Low (5-20%), Negligible (<5%). Warning: extreme yields often indicate unsustainable revenue or micro-cap illiquidity.

False Positive Filtering

Not every signal is actionable. The pipeline applies DeepSeek AI analysis to filter noise:

  • Kelp: ACT verdict, $6,000 EV, genuine restaking protocol with confirmed TGE
  • Fluid: ACT verdict, $4,000 EV, but already has token (INST rebrand); risk of disappointment
  • Steakhouse: ACT verdict, $5,000 EV, strong TVL growth but no confirmed token
  • BabySwap: SKIP, extreme yield from micro-cap, thin liquidity trap
  • DefiBox volume anomaly: SKIP, likely wash trading
